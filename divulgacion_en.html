bertsomáticas:
a dialogue between bertsos and math

Wednesday 15, 19:00-20:30
[ Library of Bidebarrieta ]

In this event, math popularizing talks and bertsos will alternate creating a dialogue between mathematics and the Basque art. The objective is double. On the one hand, as it is an event in RSME's 7th Congress of Young Researchers, it aims to bring the Basque culture nearer to the scientific public attending the conference. On the other hand, the events aims to create new sinergies between science and art, where the improvising capability of berstolarism in front of any topic.

We will offer short and dynamic talks, of only 15 minutes of duration, in the Library of Bidebarrieta. These talks will bring nearer to the public the intrioguin essence of mathematics. After each of these three talks, the bertsolaris will each perform 5-10 minutes referencing the topic of the talk. The scientific talks will in Spanish, while the bertsos will be in Basque.

The access to the event is free until filling capacity.
