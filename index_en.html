RSME's

7th Congress

of

Young Researchers

Bilbao, 2025

The RSME's Congress of Young Researchers is a bianual conference of the Real Sociedad Matemática Española (RSME) where young researchers from the whole world tied—in one way or another—to the Spanish mathematical community meet to share their more recent results and, more importantly, to create ties and continue builiding collaboration networks between who will form the future mathematical community.

After six editions in Soria (2011), Sevilla (2013), Murcia (2015), Valencia (2017), Castellón (2020) and León (2023), the senventh edition will take place in 2025 in the city of Bilbao where the University of the Basque Country (UPV/EHU) will be the hosting institution.
