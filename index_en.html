RSME's

7th Congress

of

Young Researchers

Bilbao, 2025

The RSME's Congress of Young Researchers is a bianual conference of the Real Sociedad Matemática Española (RSME) where the Spanish young mathematical community meets to share their more recent results and, more importantly, to connect with other fellow young researchers from the rest of the country creating ties and networks of collaboration between who will form the future Spanish mathematical community.

After six editions in Soria (2011), Sevilla (2013), Murcia (2015), Valencia (2017), Castellón (2020) and León (2023), the senventh edition will take place in 2025 in the city of Bilbao where the University of the Basque Country (UPV/EHU) will be the hosting institution.
