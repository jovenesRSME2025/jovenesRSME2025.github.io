Venue

Important Dates

The congress will take place mainly at the Leioa Campus of the University of the Basque Country, where the Facultad de Ciencia y Tecnología (ZTF-FCT) [College of Science and Technology] of the University of the Basque Country will host the majority of the program and the Escuela de Hosteleria de Leioa [Leioa Catering School] will provide the meals of the congress. However, Monday morning as well as the open-to-the-public science popularization activity will take place at the Bizkaia Aretoa, in Bilbao. Other activities, such as the social dinner, will take place outside the university. More details regarding them will be given throughout 2024.

Leioa Campus
(Main Venue)Campus Map

Situated in the surroundings of Bilbao, the Leioa Campus is the insignia campus of the University of the Basque Country regarding arts and sciences. Inside this, it will the Facultad de Ciencia y Tecnología [College of Science and Technology] the one hosting the congress in its majority, with the only excepcitons of Monday morning and Wednesday afternoon. For facilitating the attendance of the participants and not saturating public transit, we will offer a bus service (of which we give the information at the end of this page). For arriving on your own to the campus, consult the page about public transit in Bilbao or, in case a car was used to travel to the conference, use this to travel back and forth to the campus.

College of Science and Technology (ZTF-FCT) Faculty Website

Located in a central position inside the Leioa campus, the Facultad de Ciencia y Tecnología (ZTF-FCT) [College of Science and Technology] of the University of the Basque Country will be the primary hosting place of the congress.

Note: Although the official translation of “Facultad de Ciencia y Tecnología” is “Faculty of Science and Technology”, we use the translation “College of Science and Technology” since this is better to refer to the university building/institution. The words “facultad” and “faculty” are false friends—the Spanish word usually refers to a university building/institution, while the English word refers exclusively to the body of academicians in that building/institution.

Paraninfo [Auditorium]
(Plenary Talks & Round Tables)

The paraninfo [auditorium] of the ZTF-FCT, with a capacity of 275 people, is the main room for events at the college. Here, plenary talks and round tables will take place.

Rooms
(Parallel Sessions)

The ZTF-FCT has 56 rooms for teaching. All of these rooms count with a computer, a projector and a blackboard. The parallel sessions will take place in these rooms. Once the program is defined, we will publish the rooms for the parallel sessions and other activities.

Transport to the Leioa Campus
(Bus Service)

For not saturating public transit and facilitating attendance to participants, we will provide a bus service between the city of Bilbao and the Leioa campus of the University of the Basque Country. However, those who want to go on their own to the campus can use public transit. For finding information about the possible option, check the page about the public transit in Bilbao.

We will add the information relative to the bus service later when there are a few moths left for the conference.
